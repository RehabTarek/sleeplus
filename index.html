Why Sleep Matters?

Damages of Sleep Deprivation

  • Memory loss
  • Reduced focus
  • Hormonal imbalance
  • Risk of obesity and heart disease
  • Excessive daytime sleepiness

Solutions & Tips

  • Set a regular sleep schedule
  • Limit screen time 1 hour before bed
  • Practice relaxation before sleep
  • Use Caffeine alternatives instead
